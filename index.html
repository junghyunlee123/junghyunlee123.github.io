Cluster-Promoting Quantization with Bit-Drop for Minimizing Network Quantization Loss
Jung Hyun Lee*, Jihun Yun*, Sung Ju Hwang, Eunho Yang
International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV), 2024
arXiv

We formulated Cluster-Promoting Quantization (CPQ), which promotes cohesive clustering of full-precision weights around quantization grids. In addition, we devised DropBits that drops bits rather than neurons/filters to lower the bias resulting from CPQ. We quantized ResNet-18 and MobileNetV2 to low-bit weight-activation quantization on the ImageNet dataset.
